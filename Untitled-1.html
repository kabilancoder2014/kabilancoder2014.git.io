Major ways to earn

Choose 1–2 to focus on first
Freelancing / Contracting

Short-term projects and retainers. Great for immediate cash flow. Focus on niches and clear deliverables.

  • Specialize (e.g., React, Shopify, performance)
  • Use proposals + SOWs; ask for 30–50% upfront
SaaS / Product

Build a recurring-revenue product. Validate with a landing page and waitlist before building.

  • MVP > early adopters > iterate
  • Focus on retention and onboarding
Agency / Team

Scale by hiring or partnering. Systems and sales become critical.

  • Standardize offerings and pricing tiers
  • Invest in project management and billing
Themes / Templates / Components

Sell reusable UI kits, templates, or components on marketplaces or your own store.

  • Focus on quality, docs, and SEO
  • Recurring updates and support boost sales
Teaching & Content

Courses, paid newsletters, or workshops. Build authority and sell evergreen knowledge.

  • Repurpose content across platforms
  • Offer actionable projects to students
Open Source + Sponsorships

Build OSS tools and accept sponsorships, donations, or offer paid support.

  • Provide commercial support and integrations
  • Offer licenses for enterprise features

Practical tips to get started

Validate before building

Create a landing page, collect emails, and run ads or outreach. If people pay, build it.

Price confidently

Charge for value, not time. Use packages to simplify buying decisions.

Automate repeatable work

Create templates, scripts, and CI to deliver faster and increase margins.

Market consistently

Content, case studies, referrals, and networks produce steady leads over time.

Resources & tools

Proposals & Contracts

Use templates (e.g., Bonsai, PandaDoc) and always include scope, timeline, and terms.

Payment

Stripe, Paddle, PayPal for payments; set up invoices and subscription billing.

Hosting & Deploy

Vercel, Netlify, and DigitalOcean handle hosting at scale; automate deploys with CI.
